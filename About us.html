About Us

Production,Developer & Designer

Neo Track Media is a multi-purpose production/ recording studio in Nairobi located in Joska along Kangundo road. We started with the vison of creating an environment for achieving a professional sound system which has seen us work with clients of diverse talents. The dream of NeoTrack Media is to go beyond the ordinary remit of a sound and become an innovative center where artists,bands,songwriters,musicians and producers can realize their sonic potential.

NeoTracK Media has taken responsibilty of helping the upcoming artist get an edge to compete with mainstrams artists. Our blend of excellent quality and reasonable prices has made us a fashionable choice since we started.
